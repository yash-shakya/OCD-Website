30 DAYS OF JAVASCRIPT

Overview

Join us in an intensive 30-day journey to master JavaScript, enhance your problem-solving skills, and build impressive projects. This group is designed for passionate developers who are eager to dive deep into JavaScript, Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), and tackle challenging LeetCode problems.

What You'll Learn

- JavaScript Mastery: Understand the core concepts and advanced features of JavaScript.
- DSA and Problem-Solving: Strengthen your algorithmic thinking and problem-solving skills with daily challenges.
- LeetCode Problems: Solve a variety of problems on LeetCode to prepare for coding interviews.
- Project Development: Build real-world projects including a Weather App, E-commerce Website, Social Media Dashboard, and Chat Application.
